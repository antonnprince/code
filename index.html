ABOUT CODECRYPT

A brainchild of ACES CUSAT, Code Crypt is a 24 hour All-India Hackathon where coding meets innovation and strategy, where you think on your feet while you compete.The participants are provided with virtual coins and introduced to a market place scenario where they can choose and buy their theme and components needed to compelete the project.

The basic objective is to complete a full stack application within the track/theme of their choice. A fixed amount of virtual currency will be distributed among teams, which will be used to purchase frontend frameworks (React.Js,HTML & Angular), database to be used for(Firebase,SQL,MongoDB) and a backend language for implementation(FastAPI, Express,etc). The prices of each commodity will vary. Hence, the combinations are numerous and the coding experience will be different for each team based on their choice.
